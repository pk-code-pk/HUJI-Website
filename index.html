HUJI, formerly HCJI, passionately boosts Japan's prominence at Harvard & beyond.

About Us

HUJI's mission is twofold: first, to connect Harvard students with opportunities in Japan, including summer camps, internships, and wintersessions; and second, to introduce Japanese students to the liberal arts education system in the U.S. Additionally, we aim to promote the exchange of ideas and perspectives between Harvard students and faculty, and between U.S. and Japanese students, through discussions on political and social issues surrounding U.S.-Japan relations.
